Benefits of a Fiberglass Re-Lining

Regardless of what your pool is constructed from (e.g concrete, fibreglass etc), all swimming pools have a shelf life.
This means that after a period of time, swimming pools will eventually start degrading, discolouring, leaking etc. We will give you a free, no-obligation quote, to re-line (resurface) your swimming pool in fibreglass.
In just a matter of a few days, your pool can go from dreary and tired to looking sparkling and brand spanking new!

At a Glance

Save Money

Because Algae does not stick to Fibreglass, you will use significantly less chemicals and cleaning products.

Easy Maintenance

If repairs are required, only the problematic section needs be repaired. You do not need to re-do the entire pool like you do with Marbalite.

Longer Lifespan

Pools that have been resurfaced with Fibreglass last twice as long as Marbalite pools.
